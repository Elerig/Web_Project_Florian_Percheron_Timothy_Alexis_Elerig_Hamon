Our drones allow you to film new moments from new angles




A drone, a controller & off you go !


Travel while exploring what you want to explore !
Ask the drone to follow you when you discover a new landscape so that it can record what you want to keep in memory
Indeed, the fact of not being able to keep a photo or a film of a new landscape can be a shame when we visit a new country...
This is why, today, many travelers decide to familiarize themselves with this type of technology !

Convenient to transport, these on-board systems are there to help you visualize the landscape from a new angle !




Does not that make you want ?
We are here to change the habit of taking photos with our smartphones...
Even if the use of drone is prohibited in different cities,
these on-board systems are nevertheless authorized in many places where air and ground traffic is little used.
